About Refund

Customer Service Representative

They are the first point of contact for customers seeking refunds, responsible for gathering information and providing clear instructions.

Refund Processor

Their role is to accurately and promptly process refunds, ensuring all information is valid and funds are sent to the correct account.

Quality Assurance/Verification Team

They verify if the refund request aligns with company policies, ensuring eligibility and authenticating customer information.

Legal or Compliance Team

They ensure the company's refund policies comply with relevant laws and regulations, providing guidance on legal requirements and ensuring fairness and transparency.
